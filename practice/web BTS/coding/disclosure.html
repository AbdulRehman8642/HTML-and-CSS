Affiliate Disclosure

Markh Lights believes in transparency and we want to be upfront about how our website operates. In order to keep Markh Lights free to our visitors, we participate in various affiliate marketing programs, including the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. This means that when you click on links to various merchants on our site and make a purchase, it can result in a commission that is credited to our website.

What is an Affiliate Program?

An affiliate program is a way for websites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to products. When you purchase a product via the affiliate links on Markh Lights, we receive a small commission from the retailer at no additional cost to you. These commissions help us to maintain the site and provide valuable content to our readers.

Amazon Affiliate Program

Markh Lights is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com. As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases.

Why We Use Affiliate Links

Our goal is to provide you with the best recommendations for lights and other home and office appliances. The affiliate links help support the content we provide on Markh Lights. We only recommend products that we believe in and that we think will add value to our readers.

Our Commitment to You

We are committed to providing honest and objective reviews and recommendations. While we may earn a commission if you purchase a product through our affiliate links, this does not influence our content or product recommendations. We thoroughly research and test products to ensure that we provide the most accurate and helpful information possible.
